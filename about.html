About Me

My name is MABEKOJE OLAWALE and this is my story.
I was born on the 7th of September certain years back into the family of Ven.(Dr.) & Evang. S. Ola. Mabekoje, then Rev. & Evang. S. Ola Mabekoje. I am blessed to be the last child of the family with 6 older siblings: Olanike, Olajumoke, Oladunke, Olafunke, Olakunle and Olatoke.
It was fun grwoing up with the family, well of course, it was fun being the last born. Well, never to forget my Grandmother who was my best friend growing up: Chief (Mrs.) E. O. Mabekoje.


Education

From nursery, to primary, to secondary and now, a graduate of a University. I went from Emmanuel Kiddies Academy, to King of Kings School, to Parents' Pride Schools, then gained admission into Olabisi Onabanjo University in the year 2017 and attained a Bachelor's Degree in Engineering (Eletrical and Electronics Engineering) in the year 2022.
